Abstract

This project explores the process of readability assessment or more simply measuring the reading difficulty. We want to add an appropriate metric to the reading to help the reader to choose the most suitable one.

Introduction

Readability is the ease with which a reader can understand a written text. Readability assessment is the process of identifying the readability level. The task of assessing the reading difficulty is a well studied problem in computational linguistics field. While there are current formulas that try to solve the problem but they lack of some important features. In order to study what makes a text suitable for beginners or for advanced reader, We used OneStopEnglish corpus which has been compiled and made for readability related tasks. Using this corpus and our knowledge in natural language processing and the neural network architectures we are going to solve this problem.
